Children Series

This series of children’s story books has been written by Oluwatoyin Meadows-Oyeneye to give pupils and students the much-desired character formation, values and patriotic training. This is relevant as a national call for re-orientation with the aim of inspiring and motivating children to find their dreams, develop and imbibe the core values as a means of attacking the present social evils and their roots in our society. The stories are written in simple easy-to-read language that can be comprehended easily. The themes in the series show and highlight social values and norms. Wrong-doings are always disgraced, disapproved or punished while good deeds are commended and rewarded.
